Pitch: KlimaRadar — Portable AC Stock Alerts for Germany and France

Every summer, heatwaves catch shoppers off guard and portable air conditioners sell out within days. KlimaRadar is a lean, climate-focused side project that notifies people the moment portable AC units come back in stock at major retailers across Germany and France.

Users simply enter their email address — no app download, no account setup, no spam. The free tier includes one active alert, and a €3 one-time payment unlocks unlimited alerts for that email address forever. Users can also choose a daily digest instead of instant emails to keep their inbox quiet.

The service is supported by affiliate links, which helps cover server costs without charging every user.
