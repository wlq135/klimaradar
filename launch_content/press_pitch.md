Pitch: KlimaRadar — Free Portable AC Stock Alerts for Germany and France

Every summer, heatwaves catch shoppers off guard and portable air conditioners sell out within days. KlimaRadar is a free, no-account email alert service that notifies people the moment portable AC units come back in stock at major retailers across Germany and France.

Users simply enter their email address — no app download, no account setup, no spam. The service is built as a lean, climate-focused side project to reduce the frustration and wasted time of manually refreshing dozens of product pages during a heatwave.

KlimaRadar covers both Germany and France, with a focus on portable / mobile air conditioners. It is completely free for end users and designed to be helpful, not spammy.
