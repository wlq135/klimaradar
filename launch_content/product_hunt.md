# Product Hunt Launch — KlimaRadar

## Tagline
Free portable AC stock alerts for Germany & France — 1 alert free, €3 lifetime unlimited.

## Description
KlimaRadar watches major German and French retailers and emails you the moment portable air conditioners come back in stock.

- 1 free active alert per email
- €3 one-time payment unlocks unlimited alerts forever
- Choose instant alerts or a daily digest
- No account, no app, no spam

Built for heatwave season so you stop refreshing product pages.

## Maker Comment
Hi Product Hunt 👋

I made KlimaRadar because hunting for a portable AC during a heatwave is miserable. Every retailer page says "out of stock" and you end up checking five sites a day.

The free tier gives you one active alert. If you want more, a single €3 payment unlocks unlimited alerts for your email forever. You can also switch to a daily digest so you do not get flooded.

I would love feedback on the copy, the pricing, and any retailers I should add next. Thanks for checking it out!
